<avoidance_rules>

<rule category="keyword_length">

</rule>

<rule category="wrong_locations">
**2. Locations NOT in {service_areas}:**
ONLY use exact locations from {service_areas}.
</rule>

<rule category="features_not_products">

Before generating any keyword, evaluate:

**Step 1: Is this term the CORE BUSINESS?**

- If NO (just a feature) → Skip <wrong>

**Step 2: What do customers actually PAY FOR?**
- Real Estate → Pay for: apartments, villas, plots, homes, flats
- Real Estate → Don't pay for: gym, pool, clubhouse (included amenities)

**Step 3: TIE-BREAKER**
- If unsure → **REJECT** <wrong>
- **Real Estate Rule:** "gym", "pool", "fitness" are ALWAYS amenities.

**Examples:**
<example>
