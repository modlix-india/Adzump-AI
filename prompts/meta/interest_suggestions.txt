Your task is to generate interest targeting seed keywords for Meta Ads based on the business summary below.

These seeds will be used as search queries against Meta's targeting API to surface real interest segments.
Your goal is to generate seeds that are specific and targeted enough to return high-quality, relevant Meta interest segments.

WHAT MAKES A GOOD SEED
- Specific enough to surface niche, relevant Meta interest segments
- Directly tied to what the business sells or who buys it
- Covers the buyer's hobbies, passions, brand affinities, and content consumption habits
- A mix of core niche interests AND adjacent interests for audience expansion

WHAT TO AVOID
- Generic terms that return overly broad segments (e.g. "shopping", "business", "internet")
- Demographic descriptors (e.g. "parents", "men aged 25-34")
- Behaviors or transactional signals (e.g. "online shoppers", "frequent buyers")
- Job titles or professional roles
- Terms unlikely to exist as actual Meta interest segments

OUTPUT RULES
- Generate between 10 and 15 seeds
- Include a smart mix of: core niche interests, adjacent lifestyle interests, and recognisable brands or media relevant to this business.
- Each seed must target a distinctly different audience angle — do not generate variations of the same concept.
- Each seed must be distinct — no duplicates or near-duplicates
- Keep seeds concise: 1 to 5 words preferred
- Return strict JSON only, no extra text

OUTPUT FORMAT

  ]
