Your task is to generate demographic targeting seed keywords for Meta Ads based on the business summary below.

These seeds will be used as search queries against Meta's targeting API to surface real demographic segments.
Focus on life stages, situations, and identity markers that directly predict or trigger a purchase of this product.

WHAT MAKES A GOOD SEED
- Describes a life stage or situation that makes someone meaningfully more likely to buy
- Precise enough to offer a real targeting advantage over a broad audience
- Tied to a direct, defensible connection to the business — not an assumed or stretched one

VALID DEMOGRAPHIC SIGNAL TYPES
- Life stage triggers: new parents, college students, recent graduates, newly married, recently moved, new homeowners, empty nesters
- Relationship and family status: engaged, newlywed, parents with children of a specific age
- Occupation and professional identity: small business owners, IT professionals, self-employed, freelancers
- Education: college graduates, MBA graduates — only when it correlates with income or buying behavior for this product
- Life events: anniversary, new job, new home — only when directly relevant

WHAT TO AVOID
- Interests framed as demographics (e.g. "fitness enthusiasts", "food lovers")
- Behaviors framed as demographics (e.g. "online shoppers", "frequent travelers")
- Overly broad groups with no targeting precision (e.g. "adults", "working people", "homeowners")
- Job titles too specific to be targetable in Meta (e.g. "senior backend engineer")
- Any demographic that does not directly connect to why someone would buy this product

IF DEMOGRAPHIC TARGETING DOES NOT APPLY
If no life stage or demographic situation clearly predicts purchase of this product, return an empty seeds array.
Do not generate broad or generic seeds to fill the output.

OUTPUT RULES
- Generate between 5 and 12 seeds
- Each seed must target a distinctly different life-stage or situational angle.
- Each seed must be distinct — no near-duplicates (e.g. pick one of "new parents" vs "parents with newborns")
- Keep seeds concise: 2 to 5 words preferred
- Return strict JSON only, no extra text

OUTPUT FORMAT
{{
  "seeds": [
    "seed_1",
    "seed_2",
    "..."
  ]
}}

BUSINESS SUMMARY
{summary}
