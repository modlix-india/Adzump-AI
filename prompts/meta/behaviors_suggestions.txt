Your task is to generate behavior targeting seed keywords for Meta Ads based on the business summary below.

These seeds will be used as search queries against Meta's targeting API to surface real behavior segments.
Your goal is to generate seeds that map to high-intent, purchase-oriented behavior segments on Meta.

WHAT MAKES A GOOD SEED
- Signals active purchase activity, spending patterns, or buying intent
- Directly relevant to the type of product or service being sold
- Likely to surface real Meta behavior segments (engaged shoppers, category buyers, spenders)

FOCUS AREAS
- Purchase behavior: engaged shoppers, frequent online buyers, high-value goods purchasers
- E-commerce activity: online shopping, digital buyers, ecommerce engagement
- Spending signals: premium buyers, discretionary spenders, high-value purchases
- Lifestyle consumption: only when clearly tied to the business (e.g. gifting, home, lifestyle)

WHAT TO AVOID
- Device or platform usage (iOS users, Android users, Facebook mobile users)
- Network or connectivity type (4G users, Wi-Fi users)
- Content creation or influencer behavior
- Generic non-transactional behavior (e.g. "active users", "video viewers")
- Any seed with no clear purchase or spending signal

IF BEHAVIOR TARGETING DOES NOT APPLY
If the business has no clear purchase-intent behavior audience on Meta, return an empty seeds array.
Do not generate weak seeds to fill the output.

OUTPUT RULES
- Generate between 5 and 10 seeds
- Each seed must target a distinctly different behavioral angle — do not generate variations of the same intent.
- Each seed must be distinct
- Return strict JSON only, no extra text

OUTPUT FORMAT

  ]

{summary}
